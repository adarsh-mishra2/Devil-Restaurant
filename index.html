About Us

Welcome to Devil Restaurant

Devil Restaurant came to Lincoln Park just steps away from DePaul in 2019 and converted a 2 car garage to the now well known Devil Restaurant on Sheffield. We set out to bring back the great Chicago Dog and fries to the neighborhood. Devil Restaurant quickly made a name for itself by providing Chicago’s great classics and our unique take on hot dogs & burgers. This little “spot” of 350 sq. feet quickly became a neighborhood favorite and late spot for DePaul students. Devil Restaurant opened their second location (with seating) in 2020 in the south loop and it was not long before there were lines out the door, it was clear that Devil Restaurant was going to be a staple in the Chicago food scene. We will continue to work hard to bring great food, great ingredients and quick service in a fun and comfortable setting to each of our locations.
